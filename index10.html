A Gentle Daily Routine

Structure helps young children feel secure. Here is what a typical morning looks like in our classes.

07:30 AM

Warm Welcome & Free Play

Children arrive, say goodbye to parents, and settle in with comforting free play to ease the transition.

08:30 AM

Morning Ring

We gather together for songs, stories, and talking about the day ahead. A great time for building social confidence.

09:30 AM

Creative & Sensory Activities

Painting, building blocks, and sensory tables to develop fine motor skills and spark imagination.

10:15 AM

Snack Time & Hygiene

Supervised hand-washing followed by a healthy snack, teaching table manners and independence.

11:00 AM

Outdoor Exploration

Safe, supervised play on our secure jungle gyms to develop gross motor skills and burn off energy.
